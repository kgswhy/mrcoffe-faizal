Susanto Liman

Founder & CEO

Susanto Liman started MR.COFFEE with a simple dream: to create a space where everyone feels welcome and inspired. From humble beginnings, he built a coffee shop that became a creative hub for the city. His passion for coffee and connecting people is the heart of MR.COFFEE.

Our Mission

To make all taste coffee every day and connect people through the joy of coffee and creativity. We strive to create spaces where strangers become friends, where ideas flow as freely as our coffee, and where every cup tells a story.

Our Vision

To be the most inspiring coffee community, turning strangers into friends, one cup at a time. We aspire to create a global network of coffee lovers who share not just a beverage preference, but a philosophy of connection and creativity.

Our Story
